3. WordPress

From the Trustradius -website: “Wordpress is an open-source publishing platform popular with bloggers, and a content management system, known for its simplicity and modifiability. Websites may host their own blogging communities, controlling and moderating content from a single dashboard.” Wordpress has a great reputation as a flexible and user friendly web publishing platform. Customer base is huge, two out of every five websites on the internet use WordPress (Hubspot.com 2022).
